BIG dataVists::Summer 2015.1

04 May 2015 on big data, lecture, preparation

Dear BIG-data-vists:

To be prepared for our first session you shall, firstly, become more familiar with some (professional) networks and the way different industries and reseachers talk about the vauge concept of big data and their needs. In other words, we start understanding the various concepts out there by studing them. This is called the paradigmatic way of empirical (social) reserach. Stated simpler, we are looking for examples out there.
